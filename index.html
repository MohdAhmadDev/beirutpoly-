What is Beirut Poly?

Beirut Poly blends the nostalgia and strategy of the classic board game with Beirut’s neighborhoods, landmarks and everyday stories. Players buy, sell and develop properties inspired by Hamra, Gemmayzeh, Downtown, Verdun and more.

  • Localized properties and prices
  • Chance & Community Chest cards reflecting Lebanese life
  • Utilities are reimagined as local services like Électricité du Liban

How to Play

  1. Each player starts with a set amount of cash in Lebanese Pounds (LBP).
  2. Roll dice, move around the board, buy properties when you land on them.
  3. Collect rent when others land on your properties. Build houses and hotels to increase rent.
  4. Chance & Community cards reflect local events (traffic, rallies, festivals) that affect players.
  5. Last player not bankrupt wins.
Quick Tips
  • Buy cheap properties early and create monopolies.
  • Keep a cash reserve for fines and unexpected events.
  • Trade cleverly—negotiate with other players to complete sets.
